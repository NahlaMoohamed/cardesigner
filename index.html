Card Designer

    First Choose the shape that you desire for your card.

    When you are done, Choose the color of your card.

    Decorate your card with the shapes.

    If you'd like a text add a text box and you can change the font and the size.

    Choose the color you like from the elements pallete and click on the element you'd like to change the color of.

    Have fun!
